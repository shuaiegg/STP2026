# Product Marketing Context — ScaletoTop

> Foundation context for all marketing skills. Keep accurate & honest. Bilingual product (English root + 中文 /zh).

## Product Overview

ScaletoTop is a **bilingual SEO + GEO content & site-intelligence SaaS**. It helps businesses grow organic visibility by getting found on **both Google search AND AI answer engines** (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews).

The product is a closed loop: **Diagnose → Produce → Measure**.
- **Diagnose**: instant site audit (14-dimension SEO + GEO-readiness), competitor/gap analysis, business-DNA extraction (what your business actually is).
- **Produce**: AI content engine (geo-writer/StellarWriter) that drafts SEO+GEO-optimized articles grounded in YOUR business DNA, in your target market's language.
- **Measure**: connect Google Search Console to track real clicks/impressions/rankings per article; an activation "coach" tells you the next best move and why.

GEO = Generative Engine Optimization: structuring content so AI answer engines can find, trust, and cite it.

## Personas (B2B)

- **Founder / growth lead at an SMB**: wears many hats, no in-house SEO team, needs expert guidance distilled into clear next steps. Skeptical of generic AI writers and expensive agencies.
- **Marketer running content**: needs to produce ranking content fast and prove it works.

## Problems & Pain Points

- Invisible to the shift in search: customers increasingly ask AI, but their site isn't structured to be cited by AI engines.
- Don't know WHAT to write (which topics actually move the needle) or whether content reflects their real business.
- Generic AI content that doesn't rank, doesn't sound like them, or risks looking spammy.
- Can't prove content works — no link between "I published this" and "did it get traffic/rank".

## Competitive Landscape

- **Generic AI writers** (Jasper, Copy.ai): fast output, but generic, not grounded in the user's business, no SEO/GEO rigor, no measurement.
- **SEO tools** (Ahrefs, Surfer, Clearscope): great data, but they don't produce content grounded in your business or close the loop to AI-search visibility.
- **Agencies**: expensive, slow, not self-serve.

## Differentiation (the wedge)

1. **GEO + SEO together**: optimize for Google *and* AI answer engines — most tools only do SEO.
2. **Grounded in your Business DNA**: content reflects who you actually are (extracted once, bridged to the target-market language), not generic filler.
3. **Closed loop**: diagnose → produce → measure (real GSC performance), with a coach surfacing the next best move.
4. **Bilingual / 出海-native**: understand your business once, produce in your target market's language.

## Objections & Anti-Personas

- "I already do SEO — why GEO?" → Search is shifting to AI answers; SEO alone leaves AI-search visibility on the table.
- "Won't AI content get penalized?" → We optimize for genuine quality, structure, and evidence (citability), not spam; content is grounded in your business.
- "How is this different from Ahrefs/Jasper?" → They give data OR generic copy; we ground content in your business, optimize for GEO, and close the measurement loop.
- Anti-persona: someone wanting one-off ad tricks or pure agency done-for-you.
